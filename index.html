Project Update List

Environment Adjustments

  • Floor Level Adjustment: Modify the floor level setting in Oculus Quest to prevent the player from floating 3 meters in the air. Target height adjustment to around 1.5 meters
  • Accident Scene Setup: Replace cones with a warning triangle for accident scenes. Create two placement options for the triangle: on the side of the street at the bicycle lane's yellow marking, and 2 meters from the car. Correct placement is at the crossing before the sidewalk
  • Skybox Update: Change the skybox to feature grey clouds for a more realistic atmosphere
  • Building Visibility: Decrease the blur effect on buildings by approximately 66% for a clearer environment
  • Floor Pattern Enhancement: Increase the floor pattern scale by 200-400% to improve clarity and reduce blurriness

Interaction & Gameplay Enhancements

  • Interactive Object Highlighting: Add highlighting for selected objects to enhance player interaction
  • Custom Hand-Phone Pose in VR: Made a custom pose for holding the phone in the VR environment. Ensure that when the phone is picked up from any angle, it automatically aligns in the virtual hand with the screen facing upwards
  • Object Placement Visualization Tool: Implement a tool in to assist us in creating intuitive visual guides for object placement and interaction. This tool enables the quick generation of transparent and emissive duplicates of selected objects, streamlining the process of visualizing and planning object interactions within VR environments
  • Handheld UI: implement a Handheld User Interface for a VR environment to enhance user understanding and facilitate easier navigation
  • Teleportation Area Expansion: Allow players to explore more areas, such as going to the glass building or continuing down the street to the next crossing
  • Teleportation Control Modification: Investigate the feasibility of using a different button for teleportation to prevent accidental rotation of the view (bind to "B" button on the right controller)
  • Scene Rearrangement: Reposition the car and the injured character, placing the car where the character currently is, and the character in front of the car
  • Decision-Making Element: Position a cell phone and the first aid kit close to each other near the front of the car. Introduce a decision-making aspect for the player: choosing between calling an ambulance or attending to the injured person first (correct action: call ambulance)
